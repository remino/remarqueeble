Directions

direction="left"

← I crawl left into the neon ditch.

direction="right"

I flee right with suspicious confidence. →

direction="up"

↑ Upward scroll. Ancient guestbook energy rising from the crypt.

direction="down"

Downward scroll. A banner descending from the GeoCities ceiling. ↓

Scroll speed

scrollamount="1"

Very slow. The dial-up modem is thinking.

scrollamount="12"

Fast. Too fast. The web page has escaped containment.

scrolldelay="200"

Slow delay. Chunky little steps across the screen.

scrollamount="2" scrolldelay="20"

Smooth-ish and steady.

Behaviour: scroll

behavior="scroll"

Scroll behaviour loops from one side to the other.

behavior="scroll" direction="right"

Same idea, but rightward.

Behaviour: alternate

behavior="alternate"

I bounce between the walls like a screensaver DVD logo.

behavior="alternate" scrollamount="12"

Aggressive bounce mode.

behavior="alternate" direction="up"

Vertical bounce. The ghost in the table layout is restless.

behavior="alternate" direction="down"

Downward alternate bounce.

Behaviour: slide

behavior="slide"

Slides through and stops.

behavior="slide" loop="2"

Slides twice.

Legacy presentational hints

bgcolor="var(--dress-bg-2)" width="18rem"

The background color and width come from marquee attributes.

hspace="2rem" vspace="1rem"

Horizontal and vertical spacing map to margins.

height="6rem" direction="up"

The explicit height overrides the vertical default of 200px.

scrolldelay="20" truespeed

True speed keeps delays under 60ms instead of clamping them.

Loop count

loop="1"

I only loop once, then I stop.

loop="3"

Three loops and then silence.

loop="-1"

Infinite loop, explicitly.

Start and stop API

Controlled with start() and stop().
